// How It Works

Set once. Monitored always.

No complex setup, no agents to maintain. We instrument your stack and watch it so you don't have to.

01

Ops Risk Audit

We start with a free review of your current operational stack — identifying where your highest failure risks are and what's worth monitoring vs. what isn't. Delivered async within 48 hours.

02

Monitoring Setup

We instrument your services — endpoints, uptime checks, webhook health, checkout flows, lead forms — using your existing stack where possible. No heavy infrastructure changes.

03

Active Watching

Our team monitors alerts in real time. Threshold breaches trigger escalation protocols, not just email notifications that get missed at 2am on a Friday.

04

Incident Response + Reporting

When something fails, we diagnose, notify, and where possible resolve — then send you a written incident report. Monthly operational reviews included at higher tiers.
