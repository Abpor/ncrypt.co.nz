// How It Works

One import. Quiet tracking. Timely alerts.

We designed it to be invisible until it matters — then unavoidably clear when something needs your attention.

01

Upload Your License Data

Send us an export of your existing license information — a spreadsheet, a document, whatever you have. If you have nothing organized yet, we'll work through it with you during onboarding.

02

We Structure & Verify

Our team normalizes your data into the Lithotraq system — cleaning up missing renewal dates, identifying ambiguous license types, and flagging anything that's already close to expiry.

03

Silent Ongoing Tracking

The system runs quietly. Expiration dates are tracked, renewal windows are calculated, and you get nothing from us until there's something worth your attention.

04

Timely Alerts — Your Way

Before a license expires, we reach out via your preferred channel (email, Slack, or your existing workflow). You confirm whether to renew. On managed plans, we handle the renewal steps for you.
